Rules

1. Participants cannot intentionally throw a game. They must participate in everything attempting to win.

2. The host will be responible for the next tournament and picking the games that will be played. (minimum of 3 & maximum of 5)

3. If you have hosted a tournament before, you are not allowed to repeat your own games. Exception, games hosted in different tournaments are allowed.

4. The only people required to show up to the tournament are the previous trophy holders. If they are unable to make an appearance, the next tournament they show up to they will have an added alcohol stipulation.

5. If someone wins back to back tournament, the following tournament will also have drinking rules set specifically for them.

6. The finals of each game are encouraged to be watched by everyone.
